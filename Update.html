Our Quiz Company

Quiz Catalyst, the quizzing company, was started in 2016 by a team of passionate quizzers & quizmasters from IIM Ahmedabad to empower school students with age-appropriate knowledge.

In the years that followed, Catalyst understood the power of quiz shows in educating the target audience and building a brand. We identified the most simple, yet most-effective way to disseminate information through gamified on-stage quiz shows.

Featured blog posts

Branding Through Quiz Shows - Discover Holland Quiz
Quiz Catalyst collaborated with the Embassy of the Netherlands in India to create awareness about the Netherlands as an educational destination through an intercollegiate quiz show.
Branding Through Quiz Shows - Amul India
Quiz Catalyst collaborated with Amul Tamil Nadu division to host the Verghese Memorial Quiz on National Milk Day to create awareness about various Amul products among school children.
Branding Through Quiz Shows - Paavai Educations
Quiz Catalyst collaborated with the Paavai Group of Institutions to host PaavaIQ - an interschool general knowledge quiz to create awareness about the various courses offered by the Paavai Group of Institutions, Tamil Nadu.
Quiz for Special Children - LTIMindtree
Quiz Catalyst collaborated with the LTIMindtree group to initiate the first-ever quiz for special children in Tamil Nadu titled “Quizabled” as a part of their CSR initiative to promote education and inclusivity.
